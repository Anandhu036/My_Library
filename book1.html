Hello Beautiful

by Ann Napolitano

Description

Hello Beautiful by Ann Napolitano is a family saga, an homage to Little Women, about William Waters, a man with a tragic past, who finds love and belonging with the close-knit Padavano sisters in Chicago, but whose dark history threatens to tear their seemingly unbreakable bond apart over several decades, exploring themes of love, family, identity, mental health, and forgiveness.
